\section[Of the Shining Underlife]{Of the Shining Underlife || \emph{Carl Phillips} \hspace*{\fill}  \thepage}
\label{sec:Of_the_Shining_Underlife}
\hspace{0pt}
\vfill
\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{l}
Above me, the branches toss toward and away \\
\hspace{3mm} from each other \\
the way privacy does with what ends up \\
showing, despite ourselves, of \\
who we are, inside.
\\
\-\ \\ \hspace{25mm} Then they're branches again \\
\hspace{3mm} -- hickory, I think.
\\
\-\ \\ \hspace{10mm} -- It's not too late then.
\end{tabular}
\end{center}
